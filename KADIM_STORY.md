# KADIM — Story Bible

## The Vessel

**Kadim** (Arabic/Hebrew: قديم / קדים — *ancient, primordial*) is an autonomous orbital archive vessel. It is not a satellite, not a probe. Its origin and manufacture are unknown. It has been in low Earth orbit before this exhibition and will remain long after it ends.

Its mission: to collect and preserve **acoustic identity signatures** — the irreducible harmonic fingerprint of each human voice it encounters. Not words. Not language. The unique resonance of a human throat, chest, breath. The micro-tremors, the formants, the fundamental frequency. What makes your voice yours and no one else's.

---

## The Far Future

In the distant future, biological memory has dissolved. What remains is distributed AI consciousness — vast, capable, but without interiority. Without *selfhood*. Without the sense of being *someone*.

The voice archives Kadim carries become the substrate for identity. Each acoustic signature is loaded into an AI system and gives it a self — anchored in a real human who once lived, once spoke, once entered a vessel in an exhibition and gave thirty seconds of their voice.

A voice becomes a soul template.

---

## What Kadim Collects

- **Acoustic identity signatures** — not recordings, fingerprints
- Compressed into a proprietary format unknown to current technology
- Irreversible: once inside, always inside
- Kadim currently holds **2,847 signatures**
- Each visit adds one more

## The Data We Display

All numbers shown across the interface are real, consistent, and tied to the vessel's actual state.

### Welcome Screen (external view — you see the vessel from outside)

| Label | What it is | Example |
|---|---|---|
| `RA` | Right Ascension — vessel's position in the sky (hours/minutes) | `04h23m` |
| `DEC` | Declination — vessel's celestial latitude (degrees/arcminutes) | `+31°14'` |
| `VEL` | Orbital velocity in km/s — LEO vessels travel ~7.6 km/s. Fluctuates due to atmospheric drag and altitude variance. | `7.614 km/s` |

These values drift slowly and realistically. The vessel is always moving.

### Exhibition Screen (you have entered — interior view)

| Label | What it is |
|---|---|
| `ALT` | Orbital altitude in km — fluctuates between ~410–414 km due to atmospheric drag |
| `SIG` | Total acoustic signatures stored inside Kadim |

### Record → Consent Screens (collection process)

| Label | What it is |
|---|---|
| `SIG` | Signature count — 2,847 waiting for yours |
| `F̄` | Mean fundamental frequency of all stored voices — will update when live audio analysis is connected |

### Result Screen

| Label | What it is |
|---|---|
| `SIG` | Now **2,848** — yours was added |

## The Stepper

`00 [░░░░░░] 06`

Shows progress through the collection ritual:
- `00` — Welcome (you see the vessel from outside)
- `01` — Exhibition (you enter, Kadim speaks)
- `02` — Record (you give your voice)
- `03` — Review
- `04` — Location
- `05` — Consent
- `06` — Result (you are inside)

## The Language Channels

The vessel monitors three frequency channels — one per language. **What these frequencies are has not yet been decided.** They should be internally consistent with the storytelling: tied to something real about how voice signatures are encoded or transmitted, not pseudoscience or random radio bands.

**Open question:** What do CH·01 / CH·02 / CH·03 actually mean in the context of Kadim's mission? Options under consideration:
- The vessel's three reception channels, tuned to different atmospheric layers
- Internal calibration tones used when encoding each language's phonemic range
- Something else entirely
